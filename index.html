  • Cash killerPack-12/24 was essentially dead Nov–Mar (5 months). Zero Pack-12 revenue in Nov, Dec, Feb, Mar. The only sale was $156 in Jan. It exploded in Apr to $1,826 — driven by the $695 and $677 Pack-12 plan starts on Apr 12–14. This product sells when someone pitches it and dies when no one does. It cannot live on memory.

  • InvestigateJan–Feb dip then Mar–Apr recovery: do you know why both happened? Revenue dropped to $2,322 in Jan and $2,940 in Feb, then Pack-4 doubled in Mar ($1,763) and Apr is now your best month. Something changed in March. If you can't explain the cause, you can't replicate the recovery — and you'll repeat the dip.
